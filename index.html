NC Analytics and AI Day

2026
• NC Analytics •

This is a one-day conference organized intending to bring together academics, students and industry practitioners in North Carolina to discuss emerging topics and trends in Business Analytics and AI.

This event is brought to you by the North Carolina INFORMS chapter and NC State Edward P. Fitts Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering.

When: Friday, September 25, 2026

Where: NC State, Duke Energy Hall

Registration

Registration: $90 for faculty and practitioners, $20 for students.
